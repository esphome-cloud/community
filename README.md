# esphome.cloud / community

**[English](#what-do-you-want-to-do)** | **[中文](#中文版)**

> The feedback, ideas, and bug reports lane for the [esphome.cloud](https://esphome.cloud) BETA.

This repository is where you tell us what's broken, what's missing, what you wish existed,
and what already worked beautifully. It is operated and read by **one founder**, with an
AI assistant handling the first response on most inbound traffic so the founder can spend
time on the things AI cannot do.

If you are wondering "which lane do I belong in?", the **decision graph** below answers that
in one glance. If you are wondering "how soon will you reply?", the **response times**
section sets honest expectations. If you have a hardware esphome.cloud user behind the GFW,
the **Gitee mirror** below has you covered.

## How AI helps here

The first reply on most issues and discussions comes from an AI triage assistant
(Claude Opus 4.7). It applies labels, points at `KNOWN_ISSUES`, closes duplicates, and
pages a human only for security-critical reports. Every AI-written comment is signed at
the footer (`— Triaged by AI; reply to reopen for human review`). If the AI got it wrong,
just reply on the thread; that re-opens the issue and adds a `needs-human` label.

The full triage policy is published openly in `scripts/triage.py` (added in Phase 0
Task 0.4) — there's no hidden moderation.

### AI 在这里做什么

绝大多数 Issue 和 Discussion 的第一条回复由 AI 三段式处理:
打标签、指向 `KNOWN_ISSUES`、关闭重复问题、安全报告则同步呼叫人工。
每条 AI 写的评论页脚都有签名(`— Triaged by AI; reply to reopen for human review`)。
如果 AI 判错了,直接在原帖回复即可 —— 系统会自动重开 Issue 并打上 `needs-human` 标签。

完整的三段式策略公开在 `scripts/triage.py`(第零阶段任务 0.4 加入),
没有任何隐藏的审核规则。
